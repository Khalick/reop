A Leader Born of Migori

Hon. Oketch Salah was born and raised in Suna East, Migori County, in a family renowned for entrepreneurship and deep community involvement.

His father, the late Abdi Salah, was a prominent Somali-Kenyan businessman who established Salah Bakery‚Äîone of Migori's oldest and most beloved enterprises. The elder Salah also constructed the town's first multi-storey building in the 1970s and was known for his luxury vehicles, establishing the family as respected local influencers.

This background of "hard work, discipline, and community service" shaped Oketch's early life, instilling values that would later define his approach to business, politics, and philanthropy.

Life Journey

Timeline of Impact

Early Years

Migori Roots

Born in Suna East, Migori County into the prominent Salah family. Attended Ombo Primary School, where his multilingual abilities began to develop.

Youth

Cultural Bridge

Mastered fluent Dholuo (Luo language) alongside his Somali heritage, becoming a cultural bridge in the diverse Migori community.

Business Years

Dubai & Gold Trade

Established a successful gold trading business in Dubai, gaining international experience and connections that would later benefit Kenya.

2022

Return to Kenya

Returned to Kenya with a renewed focus on developing local enterprises, expanding into mining, energy, and agriculture sectors.

2024-2025

Political Rise & Mentorship

Deepened relationship with Raila Odinga as an "adopted son," coordinating medical care and standing by his mentor until the end. Emerged as a voice for ODM unity.

2026

Carrying the Legacy Forward

Launched major community projects including 7 water initiatives in Osiri, health insurance for thousands, and continued advocacy for ODM values and broad-based governance.
